Review of Better Call Saul

Tristan Knott

January 30, 2023

Better Call Saul is a masterfully crafted television show that has captivated audiences for several seasons. The prequel to Breaking Bad, the show follows the story of Jimmy McGill as he transforms into the criminal lawyer Saul Goodman. The writing is exceptional, with well-crafted storylines that keep viewers on the edge of their seats. The characters are richly developed and each performance is outstanding, particularly that of Bob Odenkirk as Jimmy/Saul. The show also excels in its ability to balance humor and drama, creating a unique viewing experience that is both entertaining and thought-provoking. The cinematography is top-notch and the attention to detail in creating the look and feel of Albuquerque is impressive. The show's gradual transformation of Jimmy into Saul is handled with care, allowing audiences to understand and empathize with the character's motivations. Better Call Saul is a must-watch for fans of Breaking Bad and anyone who appreciates top-quality television.
