All About Better Call Saul

Better Call Saul is a TV series that follows the life of Jimmy McGill, a small-time lawyer, as he transforms into the criminal lawyer Saul Goodman. Set in the same universe as Breaking Bad, the show explores Jimmy's journey from a struggling lawyer to a ruthless criminal lawyer and the events that led him to meet Walter White. The show premiered in 2015 and has been well received by audiences and critics alike, winning several awards and nominations.
